# Personas

## Patient Persona

**Name:** Anna Cruz

**Profile**

- Lives with a chronic condition and uses the app on mobile.
- Wants a simple, low-stress overview of her care.
- Checks vitals, medications, and document updates regularly.

**Goals**

- Understand what changed since the last visit.
- Know which readings are outside the target range.
- Stay on top of follow-up tasks and medication timing.

**Pain Points**

- Dense dashboards feel overwhelming.
- She does not want to guess which item needs attention first.
- Medical notes are hard to parse without a summary.

**Needs**

- Clear status cards.
- Calm notifications.
- Easy access to documents and care plans.

## Physician Persona

**Name:** Dr. Sofia Reyes

**Profile**

- Manages a small chronic-care panel.
- Reviews vitals, alerts, summaries, and uploads throughout the day.
- Wants fast triage and reliable clinical context.

**Goals**

- Find the highest-priority patient issues quickly.
- Review AI-assisted summaries before publishing them.
- Keep the patient timeline coherent.

**Pain Points**

- Too many separate queues create context switching.
- Raw uploads are not efficient to review without extraction.
- Manual follow-up can get lost across tools.

**Needs**

- One dashboard with patient, alert, document, and summary views.
- Consistent approval and review states.
- A trustworthy audit trail.

## Admin Persona

**Name:** ChroniSync Admin

**Profile**

- Oversees user access, system rules, and operational health.
- Focuses on the platform rather than one clinical panel.

**Goals**

- Keep account status and verification under control.
- Adjust rules safely.
- Monitor system and integration health.

**Pain Points**

- Hidden state makes support harder.
- Rule changes can affect many users at once.
- Unclear access posture increases risk.

**Needs**

- Compact system dashboards.
- Role and verification visibility.
- Clear settings and governance surfaces.

# Presentation Outline

This outline is intended for a concise product demo or hackathon pitch.

## Slide 1 - Title

- ChroniSync
- Chronic care management with patient, physician, and admin views
- Glassmorphism UI and AI-assisted workflows

## Slide 2 - Problem

- Chronic care generates many disconnected updates.
- Patients struggle to know what matters next.
- Physicians spend time sifting through vitals, alerts, summaries, and uploads.

## Slide 3 - Solution

- One shared platform for the whole care loop.
- Role-aware dashboards.
- AI-assisted document extraction and visit summaries.

## Slide 4 - Product Tour

- Patient dashboard
- Physician dashboard
- Admin dashboard
- Notifications and context

## Slide 5 - Workflow Demo

- Upload a document
- Extract structured clinical data
- Review the generated summary
- Surface alerts and follow-up items

## Slide 6 - Architecture

- Next.js frontend
- Firebase Auth, Firestore, and Storage
- Server routes for AI, uploads, alerts, summaries, and webhooks

## Slide 7 - Safety and Control

- Role-based access
- Firestore and Storage rules
- Review-before-publish workflow for AI-assisted content

## Slide 8 - Why It Matters

- Less context switching
- Clearer follow-up actions
- Better visibility for patients and care teams

## Slide 9 - Future Work

- Scheduling and appointment coordination
- Richer notification routing
- Deeper treatment planning

## Slide 10 - Close

- ChroniSync keeps care visible, actionable, and calm.
- Invite questions and next-step feedback.
